Logo Design

A contact from Ramsey County Libraries reached out to me for a logo redesign request. They provided me with context.

"Here is the deal. We have an annual used books sale to raise money for the library. In the past we have called it a pop up used book sale. We want to move away from that by just calling it a used book sale. It can be created as a stand alone image but it will always be used with the Friends logo. Keep in mind that the Friends logo is our branding and standard of identity in creating something new however we want the image to be attention getting, retail-oriented, friendly for promoting the sale."

So I decided to take a friendly approach

My initial thought process was to combine books with people. As you can see, the body of the people represent a book and also portray a humanized figure. Putting three of them together tied the knot to show the "friendliness" of reading together.
